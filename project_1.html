The timeline of che guvera:

1928 - born on 14th June at Rosario, Argentina

1953- medical studies at the University of Buenos Aires, Guevara became politically active first in his native Argentina and then in neighboring Bolivia and Guatemala

1955- He met Cuban revolutionary Fidel Castro and his brother Raúl while in Mexico.Guevara became part of Fidel Castro’s efforts to overthrow the Batista government in Cuba. He served as a military advisor to Castro and led guerrilla troops in battles against Batista forces.

1959- When Castro took power in 1959, Guevara became in charge of La Cabaña Fortress prison.

1964- Guevara addressed the United Nations General Assembly and he also expressed support for the people of Puerto Rico.

1965- Guevara left this post to export the ideas of Cuba’s revolution to other parts of the world.
